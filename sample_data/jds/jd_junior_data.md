# Junior Data Analyst

**Company:** Acme Cloud Logistics
**Location:** Hybrid (3 days in NYC office)
**Level:** Junior (0 to 2 years)
**Compensation:** $75k to $95k base

## About the role

Acme Cloud Logistics processes millions of shipment events daily and the business team needs a sharp analyst who can turn that data into weekly insights for our carriers, our operations team, and our exec staff. You will join the small but growing Data team.

## You will

- Build and maintain dashboards in Looker or Metabase
- Write SQL against our PostgreSQL replicas and our Snowflake warehouse
- Investigate ad hoc business questions (which lanes are slowing down? which carriers are mis-billing?)
- Present weekly findings at the ops staff meeting
- Pair with senior analysts to learn modern data tooling (dbt, Airbyte, BigQuery)

## Required

- 0 to 2 years of professional experience in an analytical role
- SQL fluency (window functions, CTEs, joins)
- Comfortable with Python or R for data wrangling
- Strong written and spoken communication
- Curiosity, and a habit of asking "why does that number look weird?"

## Nice to have

- Familiarity with dbt, Airflow, Looker, or Metabase
- A degree in statistics, economics, mathematics, or related (we hire bootcamp grads and self-taught analysts too)
- Experience working with logistics, marketplaces, or ops data

## We do not require

- Production engineering skills
- Frontend skills
- Senior-level experience
