# Senior Backend Engineer

**Company:** Acme Cloud Logistics
**Location:** Remote (Americas time zones), occasional travel to NYC
**Level:** Senior (4 to 7 years)
**Compensation:** $160k to $200k base + equity

## About the role

We are building the next generation of cross-border logistics. Our backend handles millions of shipment events per day across 40 carriers. We are looking for a senior backend engineer to own a critical service in our pipeline end to end.

## You will

- Design and own a service that processes 200M+ events per month
- Lead the design review for new ingestion adapters
- Mentor two mid-level engineers on async Python and distributed systems
- Partner with platform engineering on the migration from monolith to event-driven services
- Be on call for your service one week in five

## Required skills

- 5+ years of professional Python (preferred) or Go
- Strong experience with one of: PostgreSQL, Kafka, Redis, Temporal
- Production experience designing REST or gRPC APIs at scale
- Solid understanding of distributed systems failure modes
- Comfortable writing tests, reading SQL plans, debugging production issues

## Nice to have

- Experience with LangGraph, LangChain, or agentic systems
- Background in logistics, supply chain, or marketplaces
- Open source contributions
- Public writing or speaking on backend systems

## We do not require

- A degree
- Frontend or design skills
- Specific framework experience (FastAPI, Django, etc.) as long as you can pick it up quickly
